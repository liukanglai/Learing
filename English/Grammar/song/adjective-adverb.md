# 形容词

- 修饰限定 名词

- prestigious university(名校)

1. 比较级，最高级

> nothing/nobody + 比较级 + than + A(A 最...)

- no more than = only
- no less than = at least

- more and more -> increasingly
- less and less
- more or less (或多或少)

> have more grounds for ... (理由)

2. ly 结尾的形容词

- friendly fatherly lovely lively lonely cleanly ugly deadly orderly manly daily weekly elderly early
